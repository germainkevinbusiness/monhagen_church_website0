Baptism From Mondays to Sundays.


What is Baptism? Baptism is a powerful symbol of our faith in Jesus Christ. It is a reminder that we have been washed clean from our sins and that we have been given new life in him.


Romans 6:4 says: "We were therefore buried with him through baptism into death in order that, just as Christ was raised from the dead through the glory of the Father, we too may live a new life" .


When should you get baptised? As soon as a person decides to believe in Christ, he can be, and is encouraged to be baptised. In the New Testament, those that believed in Christ Jesus were baptised the same day. Acts 2:41 says: “Those who accepted His (Jesus's) message were baptised.”
