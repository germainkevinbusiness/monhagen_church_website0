Jesus Christ is : The Son of the Living God, Yahweh



John 3:16: "For God so loved the world that he gave his one and only Son, that whoever believes in him shall not perish but have eternal life."


Matthew 3:17: "And a voice from heaven said, 'This is my Son, whom I love; with him I am well pleased.'"



He is God in the flesh. Jesus said in John 10:30 : “I and the Father are one.”



The Messiah, The Savior, The Word



Matthew 16:16: "Simon Peter answered, 'You are the Messiah, the Son of the living God.'"


John 3:17 : "For God did not send his Son into the world to condemn the world, but to save the world through him."


John 1:1 : "In the beginning was the Word, and the Word was with God, and the Word was God."



The Way, The Truth, The Life



John 14:6 : "Jesus answered, “I am the way and the truth and the life. No one comes to the Father except through me."


In essence, Jesus Christ is the Son of God, who came to earth as a human, lived a sinless life, died on the cross for the sins of all of humanity, and was resurrected by the Father. Whoever believes in Jesus Christ, will receive forgiveness of sins and eternal life.
